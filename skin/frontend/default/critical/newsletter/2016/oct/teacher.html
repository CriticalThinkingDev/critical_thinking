NEW! Sentence Diagramming: Beginning

Sentence Diagramming: Beginning
Grades 3-12+ | $12.99

Sentence Diagramming helps upper elementary, middle school, and high school students understand the underlying structure of English grammar. Students apply critical thinking skills to make a visual representation of the grammatical structure of a sentence. Most students find sentence diagramming enlightening and fun—even kids who are traditionally more "math minded" begin to enjoy English lessons.

The diagramming begins with simple subject-verb sentences, but continues step-by-step to complex sentences with dependent clauses. Diagramming sentences teaches students to truly understand the finer points of English grammar which has a dramatic impact on their writing skills. For many students, boring old grammar lessons become understandable and fun!

Each lesson provides plenty of practice diagramming. After completing all the lessons, there is a review where students diagram sentences using all the grammar rules they have learned.

Vocabulary Virtuoso: Elementary School Vocabulary for Academic Success

Vocabulary Virtuoso: Elementary School Vocabulary for Academic Success is a workbook with fifteen lessons that teaches 150 words that fourth and fifth grade students should know for reading, writing, and standardized tests.

NEW! Word Roots Level 1 App
Word Roots Level 1 App for iPad & Windows®
 Grades 5-12+ | $13.99

Students learn the meaning and spelling of roots, prefixes, and suffixes commonly used in English. Learning these word elements dramatically improves spelling and the ability to decode unfamiliar words. The activities focus on using these words in context to help students incorporate each word into their vocabulary and retain the correct spelling. There are periodic reviews to make sure students retain what is taught in the lessons. Word Roots apps will add hundreds of words to your students' vocabulary and greater depth to their thinking and writing.

Each lesson has the meanings of prefixes, roots, and suffixes used to form the vocabulary words.

The activities in each lesson include:
A. Touch the Word That Matches the Definition
B. Touch the Word That Best Fits in the Sentence
C. Build the Word That Matches the Definition
D. Touch the Word Part That Matches the Definition
